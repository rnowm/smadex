FEATURES

Targeting customers via StatIQ, Experian and more

  • Country

    Filter your campaign by choosing one or more countries.

  • Hyper-local

    Upload latitudinal and longitudinal coordinates or plot locations yourself and target down to 1km radius.

  • Device OS

    Restrict the OS platforms your campaigns run on including minimum OS versions. Only Android and iOS supported.

  • Choose Apps

    Choose where you want your campaigns to run by exchange or publisher. Create white lists or black lists.

  • Time-of-day

    Schedule your campaign to start and stop at different times of the day local to the country where the campaign is running.

  • Carrier & IP

    Restrict where campaigns land by WiFi or choose specific carriers by country.
